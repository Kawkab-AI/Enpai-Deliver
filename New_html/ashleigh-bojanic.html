About the Company (From Linkedin)

Hansen Yuncken is one of Australia's leading, privately owned construction companies, at the forefront of change and innovation in the building industry. Founded in Victoria in 1918, the company has been in continual operation for over 100 years. Hansen Yuncken is a privately owned Australian construction company founded in 1918, operating nationally across New South Wales, Northern Territory, Queensland, South Australia, Tasmania, and Victoria.

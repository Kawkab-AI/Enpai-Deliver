About the Company (From Linkedin)

CulturalPulse is an end-to-end multicultural agency offering our clients and partners media, marketing and communications that authentically engage and grow Culturally and Linguistically Diverse (CALD) audiences. With more than 12 years of experience, CulturalPulse understands the unique needs of these audiences.

Company Brief (From Website)

CulturalPulse is an Australian multicultural marketing agency specializing in engaging culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) audiences. Their services include insights and strategy, creative content, media, technology, and activation through cultural ambassadors. Utilizing their proprietary platform, AskGenie, they offer cultural intelligence solutions to help organizations connect with diverse communities. Based in Sydney, they serve clients across Australia, including brands, sporting bodies, and government entities.

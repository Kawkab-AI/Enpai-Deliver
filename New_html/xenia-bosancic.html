Company Brief (From Website)

Fulton Hogan is a civil engineering and construction company founded in 1933, operating in New Zealand, Australia, and the Pacific Islands. It specializes in road maintenance and construction, facilities management, asphalt production, quarrying, and civil contracting. The company serves sectors such as transport infrastructure, water, energy, mining, and land development. Headquartered in Christchurch, New Zealand, Fulton Hogan employs over 9,000 people.

Company Brief (From Website)

BizCover is an Australian online insurance service founded in 2008, specializing in providing small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with a streamlined platform to compare and purchase various insurance policies. Their main products include public liability, professional indemnity, business insurance, cyber liability, management liability, tax audit, and personal accident & illness insurance. Operating primarily in Australia, BizCover has expanded to New Zealand and the United States, serving over 200,000 small businesses across these regions.

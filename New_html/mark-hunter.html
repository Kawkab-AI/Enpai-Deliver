Company Brief (From Website)

People First Bank is an Australian customer-owned bank formed in March 2023 through the merger of People's Choice Credit Union and Heritage Bank. With dual head offices in Adelaide and Toowoomba, it offers a range of financial services, including home loans, savings accounts, insurance, and business banking. Serving over 750,000 members across Australia, the bank operates in the financial services sector, emphasizing community focus and customer-centric banking solutions.

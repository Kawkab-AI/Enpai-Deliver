Company Brief (From Website)

TrinityP3 is an independent strategic marketing management consultancy founded in 2000 by Darren Woolley. The company specializes in enhancing marketing performance and productivity for organizations globally. Its main services include agency search and selection, agency engagement and alignment, and agency monitoring and benchmarking. Operating in the marketing and advertising industry, TrinityP3 has offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Singapore, Hong Kong, London, and New York.

Company Brief (From Website)

Bank of Queensland (BOQ) is an Australian retail bank headquartered in Brisbane, Queensland. Established in 1874, it offers a range of financial services, including personal and business banking, home loans, and insurance. BOQ operates through a network of corporate and owner-managed branches across Australia, serving customers nationwide. The bank also owns Virgin Money Australia and ME Bank, expanding its reach in the Australian financial sector.

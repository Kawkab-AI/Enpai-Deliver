The Royal Automobile Club of Tasmania (RACT), established in 1923, is Tasmania's largest member organization, offering motoring, home, and lifestyle services to over 200,000 members. Their services include roadside assistance, vehicle, home, and contents insurance, personal and car loans, driver's education, and tourism.

Company Brief (From Website)

Bendigo and Adelaide Bank, established in 1858, is Australia's fifth-largest bank, offering a comprehensive range of financial services, including personal and business banking, mortgages, credit cards, insurance, and financial advice. Operating over 400 branches nationwide, the bank serves more than 2.4 million customers. Its Community Bank model reinvests profits into local communities, fostering economic growth and social well-being. Headquartered in Bendigo, Victoria, the bank's target market spans across Australia.

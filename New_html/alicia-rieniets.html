About the Company (From Linkedin)

Defence Bank is a member-owned financial institution established in 1975 to provide banking services to the Australian Defence Force (ADF). The bank supports the unique financial needs of ADF personnel, their families, and Defence contractors. Profits are reinvested to benefit members, offering products like loans, credit cards, and insurance.

Company Brief (From Website)

Defence Bank, established in 1975, is a member-owned financial institution headquartered in Melbourne, Australia. It offers a range of banking products and services, including home loans, personal loans, credit cards, savings accounts, and insurance. Serving the Australian Defence Force and the broader community, Defence Bank operates over 30 branches nationwide, providing financial solutions tailored to the unique needs of its members.

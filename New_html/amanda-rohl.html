About the Company (From Linkedin)

Police Bank is a secure, modern financial institution offering a wide range of products and services through enhanced delivery channels and a discounted fee structure. Originally established as the NSW Police Credit Union in the 1960s, it has grown to serve the financial needs of the NSW Police Service and related industries, boasting over $1.7 Billion in assets.

Company Brief (From Website)

Police Bank Ltd is an Australian member-owned mutual bank established in 1964 by police officers in Sydney. It offers a range of financial services, including savings and transaction accounts, home loans, personal loans, credit cards, insurance, and financial planning. Serving over 75,000 members, primarily from the NSW Police Force and related sectors, it operates in New South Wales, the Australian Capital Territory, and Tasmania. The bank is supervised by the Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA).

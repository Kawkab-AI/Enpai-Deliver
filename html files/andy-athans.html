Company Brief

Val Morgan, established in 1894, is an Australian advertising company headquartered in Sydney, specializing in cinema, outdoor, and digital publishing. The company offers a range of services, including cinema advertising, outdoor media solutions through VMO, and digital content via Val Morgan Digital, encompassing brands like Fandom, The Latch, POPSUGAR, GameSpot, Tasty, BuzzFeed, LADbible, and SPORTbible. Val Morgan serves diverse client segments, including corporates, government entities, event organizers, trade shows, and advertising agencies, with a strong presence across Australia and New Zealand. The company has a long-standing history of over 130 years, providing innovative media solutions that embed brands into culture and connect with communities of consumers. Val Morgan has been involved in numerous high-profile partnerships and sponsorships, such as collaborations with Silky Otter Cinemas, Lexus Australia, Viking, Dell, and Group Nine Media, enhancing its reach and influence in the advertising industry. The company is committed to sustainability and social responsibility, launching its Reconciliation Action Plan in 2024 to create meaningful career pathways for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and commissioning the "Birrang Mawang" artwork to celebrate partnership and cultural understanding. Val Morgan's diverse portfolio and strategic partnerships enable it to offer comprehensive and impactful advertising solutions across various media platforms.

Company Brief

Superloop Limited is an Australian telecommunications company founded in 2014 by Bevan Slattery, headquartered in Sydney, New South Wales. The company specializes in designing, constructing, and operating fibre networks throughout the Asia Pacific region, providing scalable and reliable connectivity solutions for wholesale carriers and global content providers. Superloop offers a range of services, including wholesale fibre infrastructure, internet services, and network solutions, with a focus on high-speed internet connectivity. The company serves various client segments, including corporates, government agencies, and businesses across the Asia Pacific region. Superloop has a significant presence in Australia, Singapore, and Hong Kong, with plans for further expansion in the Asia Pacific market. As of October 2025, Superloop employs over 900 team members and supports more than 400,000 customers. The company has achieved notable milestones, such as returning to profit in the financial year ending 30 June 2025, with a net profit after tax of $1.2 million, and expanding its network infrastructure through strategic acquisitions. Superloop's unique value propositions include high-quality, flexible customization, and fast delivery of connectivity solutions. The company differentiates itself through its extensive fibre network, strategic partnerships, and commitment to delivering reliable and scalable connectivity solutions to meet the growing demands of the Asia Pacific region.

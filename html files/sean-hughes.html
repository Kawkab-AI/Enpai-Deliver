About the Company

We successfully deliver complex infrastructure with our customers and the community. With over 3,000 employees and professional engineering and construction teams in Australia, New Zealand, and Asia, our expertise spans building, civil, electrical, fabrication, marine, mechanical, pipelines, rail, tunnel, and underground construction.

Company Brief

McConnell Dowell is a construction and engineering company founded in 1961 in Auckland, New Zealand. Headquartered in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, it operates across Australia, New Zealand, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The company specializes in delivering complex infrastructure projects, including building, civil, electrical, fabrication, marine, mechanical, pipelines, rail, tunneling, and underground construction. With over 3,000 employees, McConnell Dowell is known for its innovative solutions and has a strong record of long-term clients and repeat business.

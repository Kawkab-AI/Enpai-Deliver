About the Company

Marlin is a creative digital agency specializing in working with causes and the charities that represent them. We help charities acquire and retain supporters through effective communication strategies. Our work encompasses advertising, direct and digital marketing, and supporter relationship communications.

Company Brief

Marlin Communications is an Australia-based creative and digital agency dedicated to purpose-driven organisations and charities, known for building breakthrough fundraising through strategy, branding, campaigns, and digital experiences that grow supporter relationships and long-term giving; as a Certified B Corporation, the Redfern-headquartered team of strategists, creatives, and technologists delivers appeals and campaigns, brand positioning, supporter journey design, websites and custom web apps, and digital marketing (including Ad Grants), with a track record across community fundraising and integrated multi-channel campaigns that reduce friction, deepen loyalty, and consistently lift donations for nonprofits.

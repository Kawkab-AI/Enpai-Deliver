About the Company

Scentre Group owns 42 Westfield destinations, with 37 in Australia and five in New Zealand. Our purpose is creating extraordinary places, connecting and enriching communities. We focus on customer experience, building more reasons for visitors to return to our destinations. Our diverse workforce has the expertise to design, operate, manage, and market our properties.

Company Brief

Scentre Group, established in 2014 and headquartered in Sydney, Australia, is a leading owner and operator of Westfield shopping centres across Australia and New Zealand. The company specializes in retail property management, focusing on creating extraordinary places that connect and enrich communities. Their portfolio includes 42 Westfield destinations, encompassing a diverse range of retail, dining, and entertainment options. Scentre Group serves a broad spectrum of clients, including corporate retailers, government entities, event organizers, and advertising agencies. With a strong presence in metropolitan areas, the company manages over 670 hectares of land, offering significant opportunities for residential and commercial developments. In 2024, Scentre Group reported a record $29 billion in sales from its business partners, reflecting a 6.4% increase from the previous year. The company maintains a high occupancy rate of 99.6% as of December 2024, demonstrating strong demand for its retail spaces. Scentre Group's unique value propositions include its strategic locations, comprehensive customer engagement programs, and a commitment to sustainability and community development. The company has a proven track record in hosting large-scale events and activations, such as partnerships with Disney and Live Nation, enhancing customer experiences and driving foot traffic to its centres.

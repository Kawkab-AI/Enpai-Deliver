Company Brief

Tracksuit, founded in 2021 and headquartered in New Zealand, specializes in continuous brand tracking solutions that provide actionable insights into brand performance. Their core services include monitoring brand health metrics such as awareness, consideration, usage, preference, and perceptions across major global markets. The company serves a diverse clientele, including consumer brands, marketing agencies, and event organizers, enabling them to make data-driven decisions and demonstrate ROI. With a growing presence in Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, and New Zealand, Tracksuit has expanded its customer base to over 1,000 brands globally. Notable clients include Athletic Brewing Company, Opendoor, Turo, Steve Madden, and The RealReal. In June 2025, Tracksuit secured a $25 million Series B funding round led by VMG Partners, with participation from Altos Ventures, Footwork, Blackbird, and Icehouse Ventures, marking a significant milestone in their growth trajectory. The company has also established a Global Advisory Board comprising industry leaders such as Tim Brown (Allbirds co-founder), Emily Kramer (Marketing advisor), and Benoit Garbe (former AB InBev US CMO), who bring unparalleled expertise in product development, go-to-market strategies, and leadership growth. Tracksuit's unique value proposition lies in its ability to offer affordable, always-on brand tracking solutions that empower marketers to measure, understand, and communicate brand health effectively. Their platform delivers real-time data through an intuitive dashboard, enabling users to track brand performance continuously and make informed strategic decisions. By automating the brand tracking process, Tracksuit provides enterprise-level insights at a fraction of the cost of traditional methods, making brand measurement accessible and actionable for businesses of all sizes. The company's commitment to innovation is evident in features like the Milestones tool, which allows users to correlate brand health metrics with specific marketing activities, providing a clear understanding of campaign impact. With a team of over 150 employees across New York, London, Sydney, and Auckland, Tracksuit is poised to continue its rapid growth and further solidify its position as a leader in the brand tracking industry.

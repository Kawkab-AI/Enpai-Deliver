Company Brief

Mulberry Marketing Communications, founded in 1995, is a full-service marketing and communications agency headquartered in London, with offices in Chicago and Melbourne. The company specializes in developing strategic business-to-business campaigns, offering services such as strategy and branding, content marketing, public relations, design, video, digital, and social media. Mulberry serves a diverse range of client segments, including multinational corporations, government entities, event management companies, trade show organizers, advertising agencies, and hospitality brands. With over 30 years of experience, the agency has successfully delivered projects to numerous clients across various industries. Mulberry has established itself as a trusted partner for major events and initiatives, including the launch and promotion of the GBAC STAR Facility Accreditation program. The company prides itself on its ability to handle complex campaigns with quick turnaround times, typically delivering within 7-14 days for standard projects. Their in-house design team offers creative customization options, from video production to digital design and social media strategy. Mulberry is committed to sustainability, offering an extensive range of eco-friendly products and services. The company operates state-of-the-art production facilities equipped with advanced technology for video production, digital design, and social media management. With over 30 years of industry experience, Mulberry has built strong relationships with premium brands and maintains certifications for quality management. Their dedicated B2B sales team provides personalized consultation services, helping clients select the perfect marketing strategies aligned with their brand identity and objectives.

Company Brief

TRIBE is an Australian-based influencer marketing platform founded in 2014 by TV and radio host Jules Lund. The company specializes in facilitating high-performance campaigns across social media platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, and Pinterest, enabling brands to build, manage, and measure their creator communities effectively. TRIBE's core services include creator marketing, premium user-generated content (UGC), and employee-generated content (EGC) solutions. The company serves a diverse clientele, including multinational corporations, government entities, event management companies, trade show organizers, advertising agencies, and hospitality brands. With a global presence spanning Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Canada, TRIBE has partnered with leading brands like Adobe, Procter & Gamble, Unilever, Gimlet Media, Amazon, and Genexa. The platform boasts a community of over 70,000 opt-in, engaged creators, facilitating the generation of more than 1.2 million pieces of content for various social media channels. TRIBE's unique value proposition lies in its ability to automate influencer outreach, management, legal processes, payments, and reporting, streamlining the campaign execution process. The company offers a subscription-based model, with tailored pricing options for brands, and provides a dedicated customer success team to assist with onboarding, training, campaign management, and performance measurement. TRIBE's commitment to diversity and inclusion is evident in its global team and the diverse range of creators it collaborates with. The company continues to innovate in the influencer marketing space, recently launching the TRBE token to power the Web3 influencer marketing economy, further solidifying its position as a leader in the industry.

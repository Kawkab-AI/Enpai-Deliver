About the Company

HBF was founded over 80 years ago in Perth, providing private health insurance. It has grown to become Australia's second largest not-for-profit health fund, serving over 1.2 million members. HBF also engages in health services such as physiotherapy and dental care, committed to supporting community health.

Company Brief

HBF Health Limited is Australia's fifth-largest private health insurance provider, with a national market share of 7.82%. Established in 1941 as the Metropolitan Hospitals Benefit Fund of Western Australia, HBF is headquartered in Perth, Western Australia. The company offers a range of health insurance products, including hospital and ancillary cover, and has expanded its services to include physiotherapy through Life Ready, dental services via HBF Dental, and pharmacy partnerships with TerryWhite Chemmart and Pharmacy 777. HBF serves approximately 1.2 million members nationwide, with a significant presence in Western Australia. The company has achieved notable milestones, such as acquiring Queensland Country Health Fund in 2023, increasing its market share to 8.05%, and launching the HBF30 strategy to drive national growth and enhance member health engagement. HBF differentiates itself through its not-for-profit model, focusing on member value, and its commitment to community health initiatives like the annual HBF Run for a Reason, which has raised over $2 million for 350 charities. The company also emphasizes technological innovation, partnering with global tech firms like Salesforce, Amazon Web Services, and Oracle to modernize its systems and improve member services.

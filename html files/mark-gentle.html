Company Brief

Canon Australia, established in 1973, is headquartered in Sydney, Australia. The company specializes in imaging and optical products, including cameras, printers, and multifunction devices. Canon Australia serves a diverse clientele, encompassing corporate clients, government agencies, educational institutions, and individual consumers. With over 50 years of experience in the Australian market, Canon Australia has built a strong reputation for quality and innovation. The company operates primarily within Australia and New Zealand, offering a wide range of products and services tailored to local needs. Canon Australia is known for its high-quality imaging solutions, flexible customization options, and commitment to sustainability. The company has a robust partner network, including over 65 accredited businesses with national coverage extending to the Pacific Islands. These partners have an average tenure of 21.5 years with Canon Australia, reflecting strong and enduring relationships. In 2023, Canon Australia celebrated its 50th anniversary, marking a significant milestone in its history. The company has also been actively involved in community support, launching the Canon Oceania Grants Program to assist grassroots organizations in addressing social and environmental challenges. Through this program, Canon Australia has provided over $600,000 in monetary and product support to more than 100 community organizations and schools. Additionally, Canon Australia has partnered with The Smith Family, a leading national children's education charity, to provide educational support to children living in disadvantage across the country. This partnership includes a $50,000 annual investment over three years to support the education of 75 students through the charity's Learning for Life sponsorship program. Canon Australia has also collaborated with Hatch, a global omnichannel commerce solutions provider, to implement a "Where-to-Buy" solution that connects consumers with local retailers, enhancing the purchasing experience for Canon's range of consumer digital imaging products. Furthermore, Canon Business Services ANZ achieved all six Microsoft Solutions Partner designations, placing it among the top one percent of Microsoft partners in Australia. This accomplishment underscores Canon Australia's commitment to innovation and delivering comprehensive IT solutions across Microsoft's technology stack. In May 2025, Canon Business Services acquired Satalyst, a leading Microsoft cloud and security partner, to strengthen its application, security, data, and AI capabilities, supporting its customers in the region and the business's commitment to local innovation. Canon Australia continues to focus on providing high-quality imaging solutions, fostering strong partnerships, and contributing positively to the community.

About the Company

Latitude Financial Services is Australia's largest non-bank lender, committed to simplifying the borrowing process for customers. We blend innovative start-up thinking with years of industry experience. Our goal is to partner closely with customers to promote financial wellbeing and help them achieve their aspirations. With over 2.4 million clients, we aim to empower people from all walks of life to prosper.

Company Brief

Latitude Financial Services, established in 2015 and headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, is a leading consumer finance company operating in Australia and New Zealand. The company offers a comprehensive suite of financial products, including personal loans, credit cards, car loans, and interest-free retail finance. Latitude serves over 2 million customers through a network of more than 6,000 merchant partners, encompassing major retailers such as Harvey Norman, JB Hi-Fi, The Good Guys, Amart, The Warehouse Group, Samsung, and Apple. Additionally, the company collaborates with over 5,000 accredited broker partners to provide personal loans for various needs, including home renovations and vehicle purchases.

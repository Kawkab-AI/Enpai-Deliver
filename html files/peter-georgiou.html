Company Brief

Mazzei Group is a Melbourne-based construction company specializing in luxury home building, founded over three decades ago. They offer a comprehensive design-and-build service, collaborating with clients to create bespoke residences that reflect individual lifestyles and preferences. Their core services include custom home construction, project management, and renovations, emphasizing high-quality craftsmanship and timely delivery. Mazzei Group serves a diverse clientele, including corporate clients, government entities, and private individuals seeking premium residential properties. Their geographic focus is primarily on Melbourne and its surrounding areas, delivering projects that range from single-family homes to multi-unit developments. The company has a strong reputation for excellence, having been recognized with the HIA Home of the Year 2024 award. A key differentiator is their partnership with Webster Architecture & Interiors, ensuring that each project benefits from innovative design and cost certainty. Additionally, Mazzei Group is committed to social responsibility, with the Mazzei Foundation supporting initiatives like the Royal Melbourne Hospital Home Lottery, where they build homes annually to raise funds for the hospital. Their leadership team includes Daniel Mazzei, Mark Supple, and Zeb Woodhatch, who collectively bring decades of experience to the company.

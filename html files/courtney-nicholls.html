About the Company

Hardie Grant Media is an independent marketing network made for today. Our agency network combines brand storytelling with performance marketing, balancing a unique blend of paid, owned and earned media for today's modern marketer. Our services include marketing and content strategy, content marketing, custom publishing, PR, events, social media, influencer marketing, creative video production, and digital marketing which are delivered by our specialists working at Heads & Tales, SHERPA, Tide Communications and Reload Media.

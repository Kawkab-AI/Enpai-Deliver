Company Brief

eHealth NSW, established in 2014 and headquartered in Sydney, is the digital centre of excellence for NSW Health, providing world-class ICT solutions for Australia's largest public health system. The organization specializes in developing and supporting digital health services, including electronic medical records (eMR), enterprise data lakes, and cloud-based infrastructure. eHealth NSW serves a wide range of clients within the NSW public health system, including over 220 public hospitals, community health services, and other health organizations. Its geographic presence is statewide, covering all of New South Wales. With over a decade of experience, eHealth NSW has achieved significant milestones, such as migrating critical business applications to the public cloud and developing a self-managed cloud solution that underpinned NSW Health's virtual response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The organization is known for its high-quality, flexible customization, and rapid deployment capabilities, enabling the swift scaling of services to meet evolving healthcare needs. eHealth NSW's strengths in the digital health sector include its expertise in cloud infrastructure, automation, and its ability to collaborate effectively with various stakeholders to deliver innovative healthcare solutions.

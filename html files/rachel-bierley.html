Company Brief

Captify Technologies, founded in 2011 and headquartered in London, UK, is a leading Search Intelligence Platform specializing in real-time audience insights and data-driven advertising solutions. The company offers a range of services, including search-powered audience targeting, contextual advertising, and programmatic media buying, all without reliance on third-party cookies. Captify serves a diverse clientele, encompassing multinational corporations, advertising agencies, publishers, and media buyers across various industries. With a global presence, Captify operates in multiple markets, including the United States, Europe, and Asia. The company has established strategic partnerships with industry leaders such as Azerion, IRIS.TV, Adalyser, Good-Loop, VideoAmp, Scope3, LiveRamp, Smart, and LightBoxTV, enhancing its service offerings and expanding its reach. Captify has achieved significant milestones, including the launch of its Green Media Product to measure and reduce carbon emissions in digital campaigns and the integration with VideoAmp to link search outcomes to currency-grade viewership data. The company differentiates itself through its proprietary Search Intelligence technology, which analyzes real-time search data from over 2 billion devices globally, providing advertisers with actionable insights into consumer intent and behavior. Captify's unique value propositions include high-quality, first-party data, flexible customization options, and a commitment to sustainability, as demonstrated by its Green Media Product. The company is well-equipped to handle large-scale, data-driven advertising campaigns, offering creative product design and premium solutions tailored to client needs.

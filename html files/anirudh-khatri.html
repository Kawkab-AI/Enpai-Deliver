About the Company

Martinus is an Australian owned, industry leading, full-service railway infrastructure company that builds and maintains large scale railways and intermodals across Australia, New Zealand, North America and Chile along with select projects in other countries around the globe. Our vertically integrated model allows us to develop, build, own, operate and maintain railways, providing long term solutions for our clients.

Company Brief

Martinus Rail has been involved in several significant events and partnerships in recent years. In June 2025, they partnered with Vitrinite to deliver integrated rail and haulage solutions in Queensland. In August 2023, Martinus North America, Inc. acquired Bottom Line Company LLC, marking their first North American acquisition. In November 2024, Martinus and FCC Construction Australia signed a Memorandum of Understanding to collaborate on railway infrastructure projects both in Australia and internationally. Additionally, in July 2023, Martinus was awarded the Inland Rail - Albury to Parkes Enhancement Projects, a $400+ million contract.

About the Company

AZoNetwork is the leading Content Marketing Platform for Science, Healthcare and Engineering companies. Through our network of Industry leading sites we bring the latest news, interviews, products, videos and more to over 100 Million Scientists, Engineers and Industry Professionals.

Company Brief

AZoNetwork, founded in 2000 and headquartered in Sydney, Australia, is a leading content marketing platform specializing in science, healthcare, and engineering sectors. The company operates a network of industry-specific sites, including News-Medical, AZoLifeSciences, AZoMaterials, AZoNano, AZoRobotics, and AZoSensors, delivering the latest news, interviews, products, and videos to over 100 million scientists, engineers, and industry professionals annually. Serving a diverse clientele ranging from multinational corporations to SME start-ups, AZoNetwork offers measurable return on investment through content creation, targeted distribution, and the AZoIntel content performance analytics platform. With operations in Manchester, London, and New York, the company has expanded its global footprint, achieving over 5 million monthly visitor sessions and ranking among the top 2,000 online networks in the USA. In 2021, AZoNetwork was recognized as a SMART50 award winner, and in 2022, it was named in the Northern Tech Awards Top 100 Fastest Growing Companies. The team has grown to over 50 employees, reflecting its commitment to innovation and excellence. In November 2023, AZoNetwork launched AZoAI, an open-access platform for the artificial intelligence industry, further diversifying its offerings. The company is dedicated to providing high-quality, flexible customization, and fast delivery, catering to the unique needs of the promotional gifts industry and B2B sales.

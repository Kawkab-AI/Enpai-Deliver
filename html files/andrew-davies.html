Company Brief

Vocus is an Australian telecommunications company founded in 2008, headquartered in Melbourne, Victoria. The company specializes in providing high-performance fibre and network solutions, including internet services, unified communications, data and security, mobile, and voice services. Vocus serves a diverse clientele, including small to medium-sized businesses, enterprises, government agencies, and wholesale partners. With a national fibre network spanning over 51,000 km, Vocus offers extensive coverage across Australia, connecting nearly 20,000 buildings and providing access to 121 nbn‚Ñ¢ points of interconnect.

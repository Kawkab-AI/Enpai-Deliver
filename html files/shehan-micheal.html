About the Company

Opal HealthCare operates 142 Care Communities across Australia, providing residential aged care services including respite, dementia, palliative care, and rehabilitation. The organization is characterized by its commitment to empathy and understanding, delivering high-quality care through experienced professionals. Opal HealthCare emphasizes the importance of creating a supportive environment for its residents to encourage meaningful and purposeful living.

Company Brief

Opal HealthCare is an Australian company specializing in residential aged care services, offering permanent care, respite, and dementia care across various care communities. Established in 2004, the company is headquartered in Sydney, Australia. Opal HealthCare operates numerous care communities throughout Australia, providing high-quality care founded on clinical excellence and nurturing residents' overall well-being.

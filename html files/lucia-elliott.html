About the Company

Advertising Council Australia is the peak professional body representing agencies in the Australian advertising industry. Our diverse member base spans over 160 agencies across various sectors, including creative, digital, and healthcare. We raise professional standards through development, advocacy, and award programs. Our mission is to highlight the value of commercial creativity in driving business growth.

Company Brief

Advertising Council Australia (ACA), established in 1910 and headquartered in Sydney, is the peak professional non-profit industry body representing companies in the Australian advertising sector. The organization encompasses a diverse member base of over 140 companies and approximately 5,000 individuals working across various domains, including creative, digital, strategic planning, promotion, direct marketing, PR, design, production, and healthcare advertising. ACA's core services include championing creativity, developing professional talent through accreditation and diversity initiatives, promoting the commercial impact of advertising, and providing industry leadership and guidance. Key client segments served by ACA include advertising agencies, marketers, and industry professionals seeking education, recognition, and networking opportunities. The organization operates primarily within Australia, with a significant presence in major cities such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, and Perth. ACA has a rich history of over a century in the industry, having been established in 1910, and continues to play a pivotal role in shaping the advertising landscape in Australia. Notable achievements include hosting prestigious events like the Australian Effie Awards, AWARD Awards, and the Cannes Lions National Representative role, which underscores its influence and commitment to excellence in advertising. Unique value propositions of ACA include its extensive network of industry professionals, its role in fostering industry standards through accreditation programs, and its dedication to promoting diversity and inclusion within the advertising community. The organization also offers educational programs through AdSchool, aiming to develop the next generation of advertising talent. ACA's strengths relevant to the promotional gifts industry and B2B sales include its ability to convene industry leaders, facilitate partnerships, and provide platforms for showcasing creative excellence, thereby supporting businesses in enhancing their promotional strategies and brand visibility.

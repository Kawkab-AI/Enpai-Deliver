About the Company

VMO is dedicated to redefining outdoor advertising. As a data-driven outdoor network, VMO connects brands with the right audiences at the right places and times across Australia and New Zealand. We pride ourselves on innovation and delivering value for our clients through our unique advertising solutions.

Company Brief

Val Morgan Outdoor (VMO) is an Australian and New Zealand-based outdoor advertising network, established in 2001 and headquartered in Sydney, Australia. The company specializes in high-quality digital out-of-home (DOOH) solutions, offering services such as retail media, health and fitness screens, on-the-go advertising, and workplace media. VMO's network spans over 10,000 screens across more than 2,000 locations, including shopping centers, health clubs, office buildings, and convenience stores. The company serves a diverse clientele, including corporate brands, government entities, event organizers, advertising agencies, and small businesses. VMO operates primarily in Australia and New Zealand, with a growing presence in the Asia-Pacific region. With over two decades of experience, VMO has secured exclusive advertising rights for prominent venues such as Market City in Sydney and Valley Metro in Brisbane. The company has also expanded its health and fitness network by partnering with major gym chains like Jetts Fitness and Zap Fitness. VMO's unique value propositions include its extensive digital screen network, proprietary audience measurement tools like DART, and innovative programmatic outdoor solutions such as Social Journeys. The company is committed to sustainability, incorporating eco-friendly practices in its operations and supporting community initiatives. VMO's strengths in the promotional gifts industry and B2B sales include its ability to deliver targeted, data-driven advertising solutions that effectively reach and engage diverse audiences across various environments.

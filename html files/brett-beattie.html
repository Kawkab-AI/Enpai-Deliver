About the Company

At Estia Health, everything we do is guided by our purpose to enrich and celebrate life together. We believe everyone has the right to be treated with compassion and respect at every stage of life. With 70+ homes across Australia, our homes are welcoming places where all residents can expect exceptional care and enriching experiences, delivered by our expert teams.

Company Brief

Estia Health, founded in 2014, is an Australian aged care provider headquartered in Sydney. The company operates 75 residential aged care homes across Victoria, South Australia, New South Wales, and Queensland, caring for over 8,000 residents annually. In December 2023, Estia Health was acquired by Bain Capital Private Equity for A$3.20 per share, valuing the company at approximately A$840 million. The acquisition was completed on December 15, 2023, with the current executive management, led by CEO Sean Bilton, continuing under Bain Capital's ownership.

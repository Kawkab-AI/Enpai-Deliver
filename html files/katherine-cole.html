About the Company

Conxxion is a 100% Australian owned and operated company specializing in direct sales of telecommunications, energy, and essential services. Originally a part of ACN, Conxxion was rebranded in September 2024, following its acquisition by Gerard Frack and Audrey Rodda in May 2024. It focuses on providing independent business owners with the tools, training, and support to succeed, creating long-term value for both customers and business owners.

Company Brief

Conxxion, formerly known as ACN Pacific, was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in Australia. The company specializes in direct selling of essential services, including telecommunications, energy, and other key sectors, to both residential and business customers. Their product offerings encompass mobile services, broadband, energy plans, and home security solutions. Conxxion serves a diverse clientele, including individual consumers, small and medium-sized enterprises, and large corporations. With over 20 years of industry experience in Australia, the company has established a strong presence across the country. Conxxion has successfully partnered with notable companies such as MATE, energyclubnz, and ADT Security New Zealand, enhancing its service portfolio. The company is committed to empowering individuals through its Independent Business Owner (IBO) program, offering tools, training, and support to help them build and grow their businesses. Conxxion emphasizes integrity, empowerment, community, and a commitment to giving back, as demonstrated by their partnership with Foodbank Australia, donating a meal for every new connection into the business. Their mission is to make business ownership accessible and achievable for everyone, providing a sustainable path to residual income and financial independence. Conxxion's unique value propositions include high-quality services, flexible customization, and a supportive community, setting them apart in the direct selling industry. The company offers a robust training and support system, setting a clear path to success for its IBOs. Conxxion's dynamic training events bring together entrepreneurs to gain insights, share success stories, and build valuable connections, fostering a collaborative environment for business growth. Their dedication to community engagement is evident through initiatives like raising over $20,000 for Ronald McDonald House Charities Greater Western Sydney during their virtual event in 2020. Conxxion's consolidation of key websites aims to streamline IBO and customer experiences, reflecting their commitment to innovation and efficiency. The company continues to adapt and evolve, ensuring that their services meet the changing needs of the market and their customers.

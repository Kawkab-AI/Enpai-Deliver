About the Company

Vicinity Centres manages a wide range of retail destinations across Australia, serving as important community hubs. They emphasize a diverse retail mix, sustainable practices, and contemporary experiences while managing over 2.5 million square meters of leasable space. Their commitment to social and environmental sustainability highlights their focus on creating valuable community connections.

Company Brief

Vicinity Centres, established in 2015 through the merger of Novion Property Group and Federation Centres, is an Australian Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) specializing in the ownership and management of shopping centres across Australia. Headquartered at Chadstone Shopping Centre in Melbourne, the company manages a diverse portfolio of retail assets, including iconic destinations like Chadstone in Melbourne and Queen Victoria Building in Sydney. As of December 2021, Vicinity Centres had stakes in 60 shopping centres, with a revenue of $802 million and a net income of $1.2 billion in 2022.

About the Company

E-Bisglobal is an Aboriginal owned business recognized as a true Managed Services Provider that exceeds expectations. We specialize in Print Management, Marketing Services, Managed Services, and Warehousing & Logistics. Our commitment to preserving Aboriginal Heritage is paramount, and we focus on reconciliation and greater understanding of Indigenous cultures. E-Bisglobal aims to increase employment opportunities for Indigenous people, fostering a diverse and inclusive workforce.

Company Brief

E-Bisglobal Pty Ltd, founded in 1990, is an Aboriginal and family-owned business headquartered in Tuggerah, New South Wales, Australia. The company specializes in print management, digital platforms, warehousing logistics, and marketing services, offering solutions such as promotional products, branded merchandise, custom printing, and packaging. E-Bisglobal serves a diverse clientele, including corporate entities, government agencies, event organizers, and advertising agencies, with a national presence across Australia. With over three decades of experience, the company has managed significant projects, including processing over 65 million envelopes and conducting more than 1 million warehouse picks annually. E-Bisglobal's unique value propositions include high-quality products, flexible customization options, fast delivery, and a commitment to sustainability, offering eco-friendly products made from recycled materials, bamboo, and organic cotton. The company operates a secure 3,000-square-meter warehouse facility in Tuggerah, NSW, and has developed proprietary software solutions like ZOOL¬Æ for procurement workflow and QOKKI¬Æ for event management, both recognized with industry awards. E-Bisglobal is a Supply Nation Certified supplier and an Assured Member of the NSW Indigenous Chamber of Commerce, reflecting its dedication to supporting Indigenous businesses and communities.

About the Company

R6 Digital is a Brisbane based Digital Agency. With experts across Marketing, Creative and Development, we are uniquely equipped to help businesses harness the power of digital across the full customer experience. Our wide range of digital services include digital marketing, social media management, search engine optimisation (SEO), Google Ads, content writing, web design, graphic design, branding solutions, professional photography, videography, custom development and web maintenance.

Company Brief

R6 Digital is a premier full-service digital agency founded in Brisbane, Australia, specializing in connecting strategy with creative to craft experiences that drive action. Their core services include brand identity development, user experience design, web design, custom development, digital marketing, and content creation. R6 Digital serves a diverse clientele, including corporates, government entities, events, trade shows, and advertising agencies, with a strong presence in the Australian market. The company has been recognized with multiple industry awards, such as the "Service Member of the Year" and "Innovation" awards from the Self Storage Association of Australasia. R6 Digital's unique value propositions include high-quality, flexible customization, and fast delivery, with a dedicated team of over 50 members. Their strengths in the promotional gifts industry and B2B sales are evident in their ability to handle complex projects, as demonstrated by their work with the Royal Australian Air Force and the development of the "Storapp" for self-storage customers. The company is committed to innovation, as seen in their collaboration with Moovv, an Australian electric scooter provider, to establish a unique brand identity and strategic rollout. R6 Digital's in-house design team offers creative customization options, from laser engraving to full-color digital printing and embroidery, ensuring that each project aligns with the client's brand identity and marketing objectives.

About the Company

Nunn Media is a wholly Australian-owned agency known for its flexibility in working differently from multinational firms. They offer personalized service, strategic know-how, and trading skills that stand out in the industry. Their independence allows for direct access to senior management, prioritizing client needs above all. Nunn Media values transparency, collaboration, and exceptional service, making them a preferred partner for small and mid-sized advertisers.

Company Brief

Nunn Media, founded in 2002 and headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, is the nation's largest independent media agency, with annual billings exceeding A$500 million and a staff of over 200. The agency specializes in integrated media strategy, planning, and buying, offering services such as commercial planning, audience and market orientation, media planning and management, full-funnel media, SEO, CRO, personalization, creative content, and community engagement. Nunn Media serves a diverse clientele, including corporates, government entities, events, trade shows, and advertising agencies, with a significant presence in Australia and the United States. Notable clients include Spotlight Retail Group, Melbourne Airport, Baker's Delight, Bendigo Bank, JB HiFi, Haval, 13CABS, American Tourister, Godfreys, Amcal, Smile Direct Club, Youfoodz, Tinder, Reece, Netgear, Anker, Harley Heaven, Silversea Cruises, and Spin Master. The agency has achieved significant growth through strategic acquisitions, including Kruse Media Services, Akamedia, Innovate Online, Alley Group, and Indago Digital, enhancing its digital capabilities and expanding its service offerings. Nunn Media's affiliation with Local Planet, a global network of independent media agencies, provides access to international clients and resources, further strengthening its market position. The agency's unique value propositions include high-quality service, flexible customization, fast delivery, and a commitment to sustainability. Nunn Media's strengths in the promotional gifts industry and B2B sales are demonstrated through its ability to handle large-scale campaigns, creative product design, and premium executive gift lines.

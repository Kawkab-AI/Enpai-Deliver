About the Company

Consolidated Power Projects Australia (CPP), a member of the Quanta Services Group, specializes in high-voltage power solutions within the renewable energy sector. With a focus on design, construction, commissioning, and maintenance of high-voltage infrastructure like transmission substations and solar farms, CPP is rapidly growing to connect Australia's energy future. The company boasts over 650 employees and offices in major Australian cities including Adelaide, Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane.

Company Brief

Consolidated Power Projects Australia Pty Ltd (CPP) is an Australian electrical engineering company established in 1996, headquartered in Adelaide, South Australia. Specializing in high-voltage electrical infrastructure solutions, CPP offers comprehensive services including design, engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, and maintenance. The company has connected more wind farms to the electricity grid than any other contractor and is a leading installer of grid-scale battery energy storage systems (BESS). Serving sectors such as power utilities, renewable energy, industrial, and resource industries, CPP has delivered over 320 infrastructure projects since its inception. In 2014, CPP was acquired by Quanta Services Inc., a Fortune 500 company, enhancing its capabilities and market reach. Notable achievements include the successful completion of Australia's first double circuit 500kV cut-in at Cressy Terminal Station in 2024 and the energization of the Hawkesdale Wind Farm in Victoria in 2024. CPP's unique value propositions include a large in-house design and engineering team, expertise in renewable energy projects, and a strong commitment to safety and environmental standards. The company is also known for its focus on sustainability and community engagement, as demonstrated by its support for the Australian Marine Conservation Society through events like the Renewable Energy Cup.

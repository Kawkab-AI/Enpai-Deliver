About the Company

GenesisCare provides integrated cancer care in Australia, Spain, and the U.K., focusing on designing care experiences for optimal life outcomes. The organization prioritizes individual-focused care, ensuring state-of-the-art treatment techniques and innovative technology enhance patient care. Their research team partners globally to introduce clinical innovations locally, reaffirming their commitment to continual improvement in cancer care.

Company Brief

GenesisCare, established in 2005 and headquartered in Brisbane, Australia, is a leading global provider of integrated cancer care services. The company offers a comprehensive range of treatments, including radiation therapy, medical oncology, and advanced diagnostic imaging, with a focus on delivering coordinated, multidisciplinary care to improve patient outcomes. GenesisCare operates over 440 cancer and cardiovascular disease treatment centers across Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Spain, serving more than 30,000 patients annually. In the United States, GenesisCare has a significant presence in Florida and North Carolina, providing services such as diagnostic imaging, general surgery, gynecologic oncology, hematology, medical oncology, radiation oncology, and urology. The company employs a team of nearly 1,000 expert physicians and has a substantial stake in the business, with doctors and managers holding significant ownership. GenesisCare has formed strategic partnerships with leading organizations, including GE Healthcare, to enhance access to advanced medical technologies and improve patient care. In November 2020, GenesisCare and GE Healthcare announced a multi-year strategic partnership valued at over USD 130 million, aiming to provide patients with greater access to leading technology and explore further collaborations to improve cancer diagnosis, treatment, and cardiovascular care. In June 2023, GenesisCare filed for voluntary reorganization under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code to advance its business transformation. The company successfully completed its reorganization process and emerged from Chapter 11 in February 2024, securing access to USD 56 million in new financing to support investment in its businesses. GenesisCare's unique value proposition lies in its ability to deliver high-quality, personalized cancer care through a combination of advanced technology, innovative treatment options, and a patient-centric approach. The company's global presence and extensive network of treatment centers enable it to provide comprehensive care to a diverse patient population, positioning GenesisCare as a leader in the oncology care industry.

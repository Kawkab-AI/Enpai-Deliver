About the Company

Akcelo specializes in delivering powerful brand experiences that engage consumers and drive business results. By combining creativity with experience design, Akcelo addresses complex business challenges and captures new opportunities. The agency has a strong portfolio that includes collaborations with some of the world's most recognized brands. Their focus on innovation and excellence sets them apart in the marketing and advertising industry.

Company Brief

Akcelo, founded in 2010 and headquartered in Sydney, Australia, is a brand experience and innovation company specializing in creating powerful brand experiences that resonate with audiences. The company offers a comprehensive suite of services, including innovation consulting, experience design, systems and technology integration, social and content strategy, creative storytelling, and customer lifecycle management. Akcelo has collaborated with prominent brands such as LEGO, TikTok, Netflix, McDonald's, SPC Global, Amazon Canada, Gatorade, Great Northern Brewing Co., and PlayStation Australia. With offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Vancouver, London, and Los Angeles, Akcelo serves a diverse clientele across various industries, including consumer goods, technology, entertainment, and hospitality. The company has been recognized with multiple awards, including Grand Prix and Gold awards for integrated campaigns and innovation. Akcelo's unique value proposition lies in its ability to fuse creativity with experience design, delivering emotional connections across the entire customer journey. Their expertise in creating memorable brand moments has led to successful campaigns like McDonald's 'Squid Game' activation and TikTok's 2024 Olympics campaign. Akcelo's commitment to innovation and excellence has established it as a leading agency in the brand experience sector.
